Welcome to TruthLens
Our mission is to provide a simple, reliable, and multilingual platform for detecting fake news. We believe everyone deserves access to trustworthy information.
  • Instantly checks news credibility in multiple Indian languages
  • Uses advanced AI to analyze news for bias, sentiment, and source reliability
  • Easy to use for everyone—no technical skills required
  • We respect your privacy—no personal data is stored
TruthLens empowers you to make informed decisions and helps stop the spread of misinformation. Our platform is always improving to keep up with new challenges in the digital world.
